Quick Review · Grammar Basics

Five things to remember.

Look these over before the quiz. They cover what you'll be tested on.

Concept 01

Verb tense matches the time

Yesterday = past. Today / always = present. Tomorrow = future.

Yesterday I walked to school. Every day I walk to school. Tomorrow I will walk to school.

Concept 02

Subject-verb agreement

One thing = singular verb (with "s"). More than one = plural verb (no "s").

The dog runs fast. The dogs run fast.

Concept 03

A, An, The

Use a before consonant sounds. Use an before vowel sounds (a, e, i, o, u). Use the for something specific.

An apple, a banana, the apple on the table.

Concept 04

Pronouns replace nouns

I, he, she, we, they = doing something (subject). me, him, her, us, them = something happens to them (object).

She gave the book to me.

Concept 05

Common prepositions

Words that show position or time: in, on, at, under, between, beside, during.

The cat is under the chair. We meet on Monday at 4 pm.
